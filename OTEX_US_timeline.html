Aviator productization, multi‑model options, and FY26 cloud momentum

2026-01-14 → 2026-06-01
OpenText operationalized multi‑model choices across Aviator products in 2026. Content Aviator offered Google Gemini on Google Cloud, OpenAI models on Microsoft Azure, and optional off‑cloud Llama support, while integrating as an agent within Microsoft Copilot and Salesforce Agentforce. Observability and Service Management Aviator defaulted to Google Gemma3 with an option to switch to Google Gemini, and Legal Tech eDiscovery Aviator documented its use of Anthropic via Amazon Bedrock with explicit data handling terms. Financially, Q3 FY26 results highlighted steady cloud momentum: cloud revenue of $493M (+6.6% y/y), enterprise cloud bookings of $196M (+29.6% y/y), total RPO of $4.5B (+9% y/y) with long‑term cloud RPO of $1.4B (+19% y/y), strong renewal metrics, and 41 cloud deals over $1M. Management raised FY26 cloud growth and free cash flow guidance, cited a seven‑figure Aviator win, internal AI at scale with a 10‑year $1B savings target, and flagged risks around the pace of cloud transition and ARR mix.
