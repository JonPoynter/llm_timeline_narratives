Acceleration: TPU cloud JV, Anthropic partnerships, IPOs, and data center monetizations

2026-01-29 → 2026-06-30
Through 1H26 Blackstone intensified AI exposure across compute, services, capital markets, and real assets. Filings highlighted internal and portfolio AI use cases (e.g., Norm AI), leadership hires to accelerate adoption, and strategic focus on data centers and energy infrastructure. In May, Blackstone, H&F, and Goldman partnered with Anthropic to form a $1.5B enterprise AI services company aimed at portfolio-wide deployments of Claude. Days later, Blackstone announced a $5B equity commitment with Google to create a TPU Cloud JV offering TPUs as-a-service, targeting 500 MW in 2027, while also pricing a data center REIT IPO. In June, Apollo and Blackstone structured a roughly $35B private-credit ‘chip financing’ platform to fund Anthropic’s capacity expansion—complemented by a Broadcom-led AI XPV platform targeting more than 20 GW through 2028 and referencing both Anthropic and OpenAI. Portfolio-scale buildouts continued (AirTrunk’s $30B/5GW India plan), while balance-sheet actions included a Blackstone-led creditor recap of Medallia and monetization of data center stakes to Digital Realty for $3.5B.

Foundations: AI infrastructure financing and portfolio energy alignment

2023-08-03 → 2024-12-04
Blackstone-linked activity around AI infrastructure and power began accelerating through 2023–2024 via credit financing to AI compute providers and renewable PPAs tied to Blackstone portfolio data center platforms. In August 2023, Blackstone led a $2.3 billion debt facility for CoreWeave as specialized AI cloud demand surged. By late 2023, portfolio entities AirTrunk and QTS secured renewable PPAs with Google and Meta, respectively, tying long-term green power directly to hyperscale and AI workloads. In parallel, ecosystem tools for private markets evolved, including Ontra’s integration of OpenAI’s GPT‑4, foreshadowing legal and operational AI adoption across alternative asset managers (though no direct Blackstone usage was claimed in that item). The period set the stage for larger-scale capital commitments, compute partnerships, and customer demand signals that would intensify in 2025–2026.
