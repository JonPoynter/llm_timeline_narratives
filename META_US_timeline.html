Open LLaMA era and cross-platform seeding

2023-02-24 → 2024-12-06

Throughout 2024, Meta scaled its open model cadence: Llama 3 shipped (8B/70B), then Llama 3.1 and 3.2, culminating with Llama 3.3 70B. Meanwhile, Meta joined industry initiatives like the AI Alliance and signed a deepfakes election pact. Financially, management lifted 2024 capex toward $35–40B to accelerate AI infrastructure. Apple reportedly rejected a Meta partnership in June 2024, signaling competitive friction with platform gatekeepers.
