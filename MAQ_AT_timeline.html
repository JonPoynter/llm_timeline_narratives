2026-01-01 → 2026-07-01
In May 2026, Macquarie reported continued execution on IC3 Super West with Phase 1 (6MW) targeted online by September 2026 and plans advancing toward 19MW of the 47MW site. Management reiterated investment to scale sovereign cloud and data centre capacity while detailing risks around permitting, power availability, evolving compliance, and third‑party/cyber incidents.
