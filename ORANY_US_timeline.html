Scaling with Google Distributed Cloud; inclusive-language initiatives

2024-01-01 → 2024-12-31
Orange and Google Cloud expanded their collaboration in April 2024 to bring AI and GenAI closer to operations via Google Distributed Cloud, enabling on-prem model execution, integrated with Vertex AI, and underpinned by significant data migration to Google Cloud across ten countries. The strategy emphasized meeting local cloud requirements while accelerating AI benefits for Orange and its customers. In November 2024 Orange announced a program to expand open-source AI models to African regional languages by fine-tuning OpenAI’s speech models and Meta’s Llama 3.1, with data processed/hosted in European datacenters. This combined regulatory posture and inclusive-language scope positioned Orange to serve underrepresented markets while maintaining data-residency assurances.
