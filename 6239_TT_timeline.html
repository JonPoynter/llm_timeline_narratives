26Q1 earnings: AI/HPC mix shift, pricing, capex up; execution risks noted

2026-04-28 → 2026-04-28

Pricing actions included broader Q2 increases across logic and memory, with steady AI/HPC-driven demand and expectations for sequential revenue and margin increases through 2026, and full-year growth targeted at high single to potentially low double digits. Risks were explicitly flagged: potential schedule slippage or yield challenges in large-die FCBGA/FOPLP, and timing uncertainties for FOPLP/CPO/optical engine ramps, where qualification or customer program delays could defer expected growth.
