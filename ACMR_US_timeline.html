Regulatory shock and initial AI-packaging push

2024-02-28 → 2024-12-11
ACM Research entered the AI upcycle with panel-level packaging ambitions while navigating tightening U.S. export controls. In early 2024 the company’s 10-K warned that key PRC customers (including SMIC and YMTC) faced Entity List and Unverified List restrictions, indirectly affecting demand. Through mid-to-late 2024, ACM announced its Ultra ECP ap-p panel-level plating tool positioned for GPU/HBM packaging and received U.S. orders for four wafer-level packaging tools tied to advanced AI chip manufacturing. In December, the company disclosed that ACM Shanghai was added to the BIS Entity List under updated AI/advanced computing export rules, cautioning on potential PRC sales impact while reiterating that operations outside mainland China were not expected to be affected.
