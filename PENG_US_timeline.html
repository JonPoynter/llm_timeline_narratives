In Q2 FY2026, Integrated Memory net sales reached $171.6M (+63% y/y) on strong AI-related demand and pricing, while Advanced Computing reflected lumpy project timing and the absence of prior-year hyperscaler hardware sales. Management highlighted five new AI/HPC customers in the quarter (seven H1) and accelerating inference/agentic AI deployments, including a Tier 1 financial institution deploying a CXL-based KV cache server.
