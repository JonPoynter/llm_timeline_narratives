Foundation of GenAI Alliances (Microsoft, AWS, Google)

2023-08-09 → 2024-02-07
Kyndryl initiated its enterprise generative AI push through strategic hyperscaler alliances. In August 2023, it deepened collaboration with Microsoft to speed enterprise-grade generative AI adoption, explicitly leveraging Microsoft 365 Copilot and Azure OpenAI Service, and scaling talent via Kyndryl University for Microsoft. In November 2023, Kyndryl signed a multi-year Strategic Collaboration Agreement (SCA) with AWS to co-create and deploy generative AI and ML solutions, including an Innovation Factory and a major certification ramp. By February 2024, Kyndryl expanded its partnership with Google Cloud to deliver enterprise-ready generative AI solutions, explicitly incorporating Gemini models and operationalizing deployments with a new LLM Operations Framework. This phase established a multi-cloud, multi-model orientation across Microsoft/OpenAI, AWS, and Google/Gemini.
