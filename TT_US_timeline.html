Foundational AI pilots and early collaborations

2022-11-11 → 2024-09-06
Trane Technologies began engaging directly with leading AI research and control systems in the built environment. In late 2022, DeepMind/Google ran live reinforcement-learning experiments on commercial cooling in partnership with Trane, reporting meaningful energy savings in real-world facilities. In 2023, Trane announced a technology collaboration with BrainBox AI to pair Trane’s Tracer SC+ building automation with autonomous decarbonizing AI. By 2024 Trane had also deployed an internal generative AI Personal Assistant Tool (PAT) using OpenAI’s GPT models on Microsoft Azure, making it available to more than 40,000 employees and evaluating outputs with GPT‑4. These steps established both external and internal AI capabilities that later underpinned productization.
