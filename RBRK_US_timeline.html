Foundation and early GenAI integrations

By late 2024, Rubrik disclosed Annapurna, an API service intended to connect Rubrik-governed data to foundation models via Microsoft Azure OpenAI or Amazon Bedrock, signaling a multi-model, multi-cloud approach for enterprise GenAI development and governance.
