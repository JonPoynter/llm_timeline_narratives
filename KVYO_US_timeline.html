Foundations of Klaviyo AI and MCP server

Klaviyo formally launched its consolidated AI offering (“Klaviyo AI”) in February 2024, packaging predictive and generative capabilities such as Segments AI, Email AI, and Forms AI into a coherent product push aimed at revenue lift and better customer experiences. This marked the company's thematic commitment to embedding AI across its B2C CRM suite rather than treating AI as a standalone add‑on. In 2025, Klaviyo advanced its AI platform strategy with general availability of its enhanced Model Context Protocol (MCP) server to connect external AI tools directly to Klaviyo data, and it released help‑center guidance for its AI‑powered SMS assistant.
