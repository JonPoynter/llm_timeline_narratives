In early 2026, Lotes cited strengthening AI/server demand, with Taiwan media noting strong CSP pull-through and double-digit shipment growth expectations. MoneyDJ further reported Lotes’ rising AI-server mix, participation in the NVIDIA AI Factory MGX ecosystem ceremony, platform transitions (SP5→SP7), expected ASP uplift, continued server shipment growth through 2027, and ongoing capacity investments in China and Vietnam.
