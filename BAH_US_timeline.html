This milestone established the firm’s edge-AI credibility for defense and space use cases, foreshadowing subsequent demonstrations and integrations with open‑weight model families.

This period set the prerequisites for later multi-cloud, multi-model offerings, establishing Booz Allen’s credentials to implement AI in complex, sensitive environments prior to the surge in frontier-model adoption across the public sector.
