Positioning into AI-led WFE cycles

2024-10-30 → 2026-03-12
ASM International signaled early that AI was the primary end-market driver for the semiconductor industry, emphasizing demand in advanced logic/foundry (Gate-all-around at 2nm class nodes) and high-bandwidth memory (HBM) in DRAM. Management commentary in late 2024 highlighted broadening data center growth tied to AI workloads and customers discussing substantial 2nm demand. By March 2026, the company’s Annual Report reiterated ASM’s focus on leading-edge logic/foundry and memory device segments, while also noting participation in power/analog and wafer manufacturing markets. The report underscores ASM’s positioning to support leading semiconductor manufacturers as leading-edge solutions continue to grow, setting the stage for the AI-driven dynamics that would become explicit in Q1 2026 disclosures.
