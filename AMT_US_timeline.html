Pre-genAI cloud interconnect build-out and campus expansions

2021-07-20 → 2023-12-12
American Tower’s CoreSite subsidiary deepened its role as a neutral interconnection platform for hyperscale clouds ahead of the generative AI surge. In 2021, CoreSite brought Google Cloud Dedicated Interconnect on-net to its Silicon Valley campus and expanded Partner Interconnect access via the Open Cloud Exchange, enabling private, low-latency connectivity up to 100 Gbps. Through 2023, CoreSite enhanced its software-defined networking capabilities to offer higher-bandwidth virtual connections with Google Cloud (20G and 50G) to support AI/ML and other high-bandwidth applications. It also announced major campus expansions in New York and Denver—adding 685,000 square feet—to address growing capacity and power demand from public and private cloud providers and enterprises, explicitly citing increased AI adoption.
