Foundations: Renewable programs enabling hyperscale buyers

2018-04-09 → 2020-01-23
Southern Company’s Georgia Power established early frameworks to serve data center and large enterprise renewable demand. In April 2018, Georgia Power launched the C&I REDI program, under which Google and other corporates subscribed to new solar resources, including 78.8 MW for Google’s Douglas County data center and a total of 177 MW of new projects with 30-year PPAs. These constructs directly aligned utility offerings with hyperscale buyers’ clean energy goals. In January 2020, Georgia Power filed for approval of the Customer Renewable Supply Procurement (CRSP) program and related tariffs with the Georgia PSC, formalizing a scalable mechanism for long-term renewable subscriptions by new and existing large-load customers.
