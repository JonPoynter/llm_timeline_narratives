2026-05-05 → 2026-05-05
Winbond’s 26Q1 disclosure underscored AI‑adjacent demand as a central driver, citing a structural supply gap in legacy DRAM (DDR4/LPDDR4) and code‑storage flash as peers prioritize HBM/DDR5/3D NAND. Management highlighted order visibility into 2027 for AI server/data center, rising DRAM contract pricing into Q2, and a mix shift toward AI‑exposed products and customers. Financially, Winbond planned ~NT$40.5bn 2026 memory capex (cash basis), recorded NT$23.9bn convertible bond inflows in Q1, and generated NT$9.45bn free cash flow. Management also flagged the risk that strong AI demand could crowd out some non‑AI applications near term.
