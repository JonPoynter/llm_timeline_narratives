2026-01-01 → 2026-06-30
SAP’s Q1 2026 results underscored cloud momentum tied to Business AI, while management detailed a more agentic product roadmap, open APIs with IP protection, and a gradual evolution toward consumption-linked revenue. The company announced an agreement to acquire Reltio to make data AI-ready, highlighted cross-functional 'All-in on AI' teams, and maintained operating leverage expectations despite AI investments. SAP documentation also emphasized output filtering requirements for select models (e.g., DeepSeek) and enumerated multi-cloud model endpoints (Azure OpenAI, GCP Vertex AI, AWS Bedrock), reinforcing a multi-model hedge.
