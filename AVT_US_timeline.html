FY26 momentum: customer AI adoption, internal enablement, and component tailwinds

2026-01-13 → 2026-04-29
Avnet’s 2026 period shows both customer and internal traction around AI. Its fifth annual Avnet Insights survey reported engineers increasingly leveraging LLMs during design, with named usage of ChatGPT and Google Gemini, while expressing preference for better domain-trained tools. Internally, Avnet’s CIO discussed structured upskilling and deployment of productivity tools such as ChatGPT across the organization. Financially and operationally, the April 2026 quarter highlighted expanding AI/data center exposure (now about 10%–15% of sales going directly into data centers), growing backlog and above-parity book-to-bill, extended lead times, and significant memory pricing tailwinds. Management also addressed risks such as double-ordering, supply tightness (especially memory), and indicated continued inventory investment (including significant SKU additions at Farnell) to support IP&E and semiconductor breadth.
