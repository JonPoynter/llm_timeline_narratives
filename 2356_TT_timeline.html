Foundation: Open Compute participation and ZT equity tie-up

2013-05-24 → 2019-06-25
Inventec signaled long-term alignment with hyperscale data center trends by joining Facebook’s Open Compute Project Taiwan in 2013, emphasizing energy-efficient server hardware for large data centers. This positioned the company within an ecosystem focused on open, scalable infrastructure for cloud workloads. In 2019, Inventec’s board approved a strategic USD 54.81 million equity investment for a 10% stake in ZT Group International, a U.S.-based systems integrator. The move aimed to deepen server market share and open channels into major cloud supply chains, laying groundwork for later AI server exposure.
