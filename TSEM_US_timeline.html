2023-09-05 → 2024-11-26
Tower established a strategic capacity corridor with Intel Foundry Services in September 2023, planning up to $300 million of tool investments at Intel's Fab 11X in New Mexico. The arrangement adds over 600,000 photo layers per month of 300mm capability for Tower's advanced analog flows, positioning the company for anticipated AI and datacenter demand. By late 2024, Tower advanced its silicon photonics roadmap: management reported the production ramp of 1.6-terabit products for multiple lead customers and asserted leadership in SiPho foundry volume. In November 2024, Tower announced volume production of 1.6 Tbps optical transceivers and released a 300mm SiPho process as a standard foundry offering, complementing its high-volume 200mm PH18 platform.
