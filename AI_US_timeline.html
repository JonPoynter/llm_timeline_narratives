Foundational Cloud Alliances and Initial Generative AI Launch

2021-09-01 → 2023-12-31
C3.ai established a deep go-to-market and product alignment with Google Cloud beginning in 2021, enabling co-selling and native operation of C3 AI applications on Google Cloud infrastructure and services including Kubernetes Engine, BigQuery, and Vertex AI. In 2022, the firms expanded the partnership under a three-year framework to scale joint selling, co-development, and customer pilots. In 2023, C3.ai launched the C3 Generative AI Product Suite, initially for Enterprise Search, integrating leading large language models (LLMs) from OpenAI and Google. The suite rapidly moved into hyperscaler distribution, appearing on Google Cloud Marketplace in May 2023 and on AWS Marketplace days later. By September 2023, C3.ai emphasized LLM-agnostic support across multiple frontier and open models (e.g., OpenAI GPT-3.5, Google PaLM 2, Anthropic Claude 2 via Bedrock, Meta Llama 2), and introduced a standardized deployment motion and pricing, including a 12‑week production timeline at $250,000.
