2026-01-16 → 2026-05-06
Riot signed a 10-year data center lease with AMD for an initial 25 MW at Rockdale, with expansion pathways up to 200 MW. The company also acquired 200 acres at Rockdale for $96 million funded by Bitcoin sales. By Q1 2026, Riot delivered the first 5 MW to AMD; AMD exercised an additional 25 MW option to reach 50 MW contracted, and an April amendment to the lease added the 25 MW expansion. Q1 2026 filings outlined ~$636m total AMD lease-and-option contract value with estimated ~$510m NOI over the initial term, phasing milestones for additional capacity, robust leasing pipeline commentary, and operating metrics (data center revenue $33.2m). The quarter also reflected significant cash movements (BTC sales, capex), leverage (~$853.7m principal debt), negative free cash flow, and risk items including a $51.9m power derivative loss and $326.7m BTC fair value loss.
