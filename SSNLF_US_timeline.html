Internal posture shift: GenAI restrictions and Samsung Gauss

2023-05-02 → 2023-11-16
Samsung initially moved to restrict employee use of external generative AI tools in May 2023 after an internal data incident, citing security concerns while it evaluated safe-use frameworks. This risk posture limited exposure to frontier LLMs like ChatGPT and Google Bard inside the company while Samsung explored internal alternatives. By November 2023, Samsung publicly showcased its in‑house Samsung Gauss suite (Language, Code, Image) for internal productivity use, signaling a parallel internal capability track as it reassessed the role of external models. This set the stage for later, broader integrations on consumer devices and eventual enterprise use of third‑party models.
