Governed, agentic AI era: Trusted AI posture, Harvey/Anthropic, Celeste launch and Q3’26 adoption

2026-01-15 → 2026-05-12
Entering 2026, Intapp articulated a governed AI posture, documenting data handling for Azure OpenAI (e.g., stateless processing and copyright surfacing considerations) and its shared-model approach. The firm moved to deepen compliance and governance by planning a strategic partnership with Harvey to embed ethical wall enforcement into Harvey’s enterprise AI. In parallel, Intapp announced a collaboration with Anthropic to build industry-specific, Claude-powered expert agents for highly regulated professional firms. On February 25, 2026, Intapp launched Celeste, a purpose-built agentic AI platform that integrates across Intapp applications and firm systems, connecting directly to Claude and complementing Microsoft Copilot users with governed access to firm data. Sub-processor disclosures in April 2026 underscored a multi-model, deployment-flexible approach, noting Amazon AWS Bedrock hosting Anthropic models and the option for customers to select Anthropic or Azure OpenAI models. In its May 2026 quarter, management highlighted Celeste introduction, early client adoption (including Ropes & Gray), contribution to net new bookings (~15%), and a model‑agnostic strategy spanning OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, and Google — while also noting execution and compliance risks. A contemporaneous Anthropic corporate update on secondary share transfer restrictions offered context on provider governance dynamics.

Azure OpenAI integrations and early Assist rollouts

2023-10-27 → 2024-08-12
Intapp’s AI journey during this period centered on embedding Microsoft Azure OpenAI into core workflows for professional and financial services firms. A Microsoft customer story in late 2023 detailed how Azure OpenAI and GPT are used to bolster DealCloud and automate meeting summarization and deal marketing/prospecting. In early 2024, Intapp branded and advanced these capabilities under the Intapp Assist umbrella, announcing general availability for DealCloud. Client adoption followed, with Enventure selecting DealCloud alongside Intapp Assist to modernize deal management and IR, explicitly citing the use of Azure OpenAI. By August 2024, Intapp expanded Assist’s footprint with general availability for Terms, introducing Ask Intapp in Microsoft Teams to help professionals comply with client contractual obligations. These developments established the base of AI-enhanced productivity and workflow automation that would later evolve into Intapp’s governed, agentic AI strategy.
