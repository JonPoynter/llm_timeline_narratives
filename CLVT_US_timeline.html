Foundational GenAI Partnerships and Early Productization

2023-06-22 → 2024-09-04
Clarivate began formalizing its generative AI strategy in mid-2023 through an external model partnership and early workflow integrations. The company announced a strategic partnership with AI21 Labs and, shortly after, documented an internal interface (Rowan AI Chat) leveraging the OpenAI API within its patent drafting software, signaling pragmatic experimentation tied to core IP drafting workflows. In 2024, Clarivate advanced from experiments to user-facing productization with the launch of the Generative AI-Powered Web of Science Research Assistant, aimed at simplifying discovery via natural language search. Together, these moves established groundwork for broader, multi-model distribution and set expectations for AI-enhanced research and IP experiences across the portfolio.
