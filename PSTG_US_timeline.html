Scaling AI infrastructure with Meta’s Research SuperCluster

2022-01-24 → 2023-05-18
Everpure supplied storage for Meta’s AI Research SuperCluster (RSC), one of the fastest AI supercomputers at launch. The deployment featured Pure FlashArray and FlashBlade at exascale-adjacent levels, supporting Meta’s training workloads across text, images, and video. With the second-phase buildout in 2023, Meta detailed the RSC at 16,000 NVIDIA A100 GPUs and an AirStore environment using partners including Pure Storage, with 80 PB of cache and over a half-exabyte of bulk storage. This engagement showcased Everpure’s ability to serve hyperscale AI training infrastructure for a frontier AI provider (Meta/Llama).
