Platformization and Marketplace Acceleration

2025-04-18 → 2025-12-09
In 2025, EPAM shifted from groundwork to packaged solutions and platform capabilities. Google Cloud named EPAM among delivery partners for mainframe modernization solutions powered by Gemini models. EPAM released DIAL 3.0, an open-source, vendor-agnostic GenAI platform designed to govern and orchestrate multi-model enterprise AI deployments across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini and others. EPAM also launched seven production-ready AI agents on Google Cloud Marketplace built on Gemini Enterprise, creating a direct distribution and monetization path through a major cloud channel. In parallel, EPAM published security guidance highlighting risks in self-hosted, open LLMs, including DeepSeek’s R1, underscoring its advisory role on safe AI deployment.
