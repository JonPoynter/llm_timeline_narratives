Open networking groundwork and hyperscaler alignments

2019-03-14 → 2021-10-27
Arista’s AI-relevant groundwork traces to open networking and hyperscaler co-development. In March 2019, Facebook (now Meta) publicly highlighted multi-sourcing flexibility across internally developed systems and Arista platforms, signaling hyperscaler willingness to run their own network operating systems (FBOSS) as well as Arista EOS across tiers. This set an early precedent for large-scale, open Ethernet fabrics used in data center and AI contexts. By October 2021, Arista formalized a distribution/adjacency with Google Cloud as a Network Connectivity Center partner via CloudEOS. This strengthened multi-cloud positioning and provided an integration channel into Google Cloud networking environments relevant to data movement for AI workloads.
