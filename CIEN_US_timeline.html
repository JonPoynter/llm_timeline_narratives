Pre-AI groundwork and cloud/edge partnering

2021-02-08 → 2023-12-31
Ciena laid foundational relationships and deployments supporting hyperscaler and webscale connectivity ahead of the current AI buildout. In 2021, the company powered Bulk Fiber Networks’ Havfrue transatlantic submarine cable and formalized a Google Cloud edge partnership to deliver its Adaptive Network and automation software via Anthos and Google’s edge network. These steps positioned Ciena’s optical and automation portfolio within cloud ecosystems and large-scale subsea routes that would later be leveraged as AI traffic and data center interconnect needs inflected upward.
