Foundation of Azure China partnership

2013-05-22 → 2018-03-14
VNET (then 21Vianet) established itself as Microsoft’s local operating partner for Azure and Office 365 in China, launching Azure in China in 2013 and reinforcing the relationship in 2018. The partnership positioned VNET as the operator and customer-facing entity for Azure in China, leveraging its carrier-neutral data center footprint. By 2018, the companies extended their cooperation after several years of triple-digit growth in cloud services, with Azure in China serving over 100,000 enterprise customers and millions of users across enterprise and education. This set the groundwork for subsequent AI-service availability through Azure-operated regions in China.
