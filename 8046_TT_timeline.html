AI substrate strategy articulation

2024-05-03 → 2024-12-31
Nan Ya Printed Circuit Board used its 2024 Annual Report to explicitly tie its product roadmap to the generative AI upcycle. The company also stated an intention to enter the AI server supply chain for related PCB to strengthen product mix and profitability. This phase marks strategic positioning rather than named design wins. It set the foundation for later capacity moves and customer engagement around advanced IC substrates used in AI servers and networking equipment.
