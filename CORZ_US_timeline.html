Foundational CoreWeave HPC contracts and option ramp

2024-03-06 → 2024-10-22
Core Scientific began its pivot to high‑density AI/HPC colocation by signing and expanding long‑duration hosting agreements with CoreWeave. The relationship started with a 16 MW Austin, TX contract carrying potential revenue of more than $100 million and quickly scaled to approximately 200 MW of 12‑year hosting capacity with estimated cumulative revenue of more than $3.5 billion. These announcements established the economic base for Core Scientific’s AI/HPC colocation pivot, with NVIDIA GPU‑oriented builds at multiple owned sites and CoreWeave‑funded site conversion capex under the initial 200 MW agreements. The cadence of option exercises throughout 2024 materially increased the backlog visibility and duration of Core Scientific’s AI‑linked cash flows.
