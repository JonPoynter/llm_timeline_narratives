Standards groundwork and ORV3 context

2022-09-23 → 2024-06-03
BizLink’s data center positioning is closely tied to open standards that shape rack and power architectures. In late 2022 Google contributed a detailed implementation of the Open Rack Frame V3 specification to the Open Compute Project, reinforcing ORV3’s interoperability goals. In mid-2024, Meta’s ORV3 liquid cooling specification advanced blind-mate, liquid-cooled rack designs. These standardization milestones frame BizLink’s later launches of ORV3-aligned power and interconnect offerings, and set expectations for customer switching flexibility and multi-vendor compatibility in high-density AI/HPC deployments.
