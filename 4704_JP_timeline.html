Foundational AI security offerings and governance controls

2024-08-01 → 2025-03-31
Trend Micro introduced governance and protection for generative AI usage with the Japan launch of Trend Vision One - Zero Trust Secure Access - AI Service Access, explicitly covering popular public services like ChatGPT (OpenAI) and Gemini (Google). The solution focuses on preventing prompt injection attacks and blocking inadvertent data leakage when employees interact with public AI tools. In early 2025, the company open-sourced Trend Cybertron, a specialized AI model and agent framework for autonomous cybersecurity agents. Cybertron was fine-tuned on Llama 3.1 and engineered for deployment on NVIDIA infrastructure (NIM and DGX), signaling an internal capability build-out and optionality around open models and accelerated inference.
