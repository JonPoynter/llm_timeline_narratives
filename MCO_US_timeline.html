Foundation: Microsoft/OpenAI and Google Cloud partnerships; Research Assistant commercialization

2023-06-29 → 2024-06-27
Moody’s initiated a strategic pivot into generative AI with Microsoft in mid‑2023, anchoring solutions on Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service and deploying “Moody’s CoPilot” internally to 14,000 employees, while previewing a customer‑facing Moody’s Research Assistant. By December 2023, Moody’s Research Assistant launched commercially as an add‑on to CreditView, explicitly powered by Microsoft’s Azure OpenAI Service and positioned to materially reduce time spent on data collection and analysis. In parallel, Moody’s formed a Google Cloud partnership (Oct 2023) to co‑create gen‑AI applications on Vertex AI, expose proprietary datasets in BigQuery, and enhance enterprise search with Vertex AI Search. In June 2024, Google announced expanded grounding capabilities on Vertex AI — including third‑party datasets — citing Moody’s as a specialized provider, signaling an additional distribution and workflow channel for Moody’s data in Google’s AI ecosystem.
