2025-10-27 → 2025-12-08

Concurrently, NextEra and Meta reached a 2.5 GW milestone across 13 projects (11 PPAs and two ESAs) scheduled to come online between 2026 and 2028, underscoring broad-based hyperscaler demand for large, clean, and increasingly dispatchable energy resources that underpin AI workloads.
