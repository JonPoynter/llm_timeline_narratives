Operational AI adoption and a Chrome root trust setback

2025-02-27 → 2025-06-25
In early 2025, Chunghwa Telecom expanded internal use of Google Cloud AI. A Google Cloud case described CHT using tools like NotebookLM Enterprise for decision support, a Gemini 2.0 customer agent projected to cut billing-related calls by 25% annually, and Document AI to automate roaming charge verification. Foundational Google Cloud connectivity and partnership

2019-04-12 → 2021-06-17
Chunghwa Telecom established an early technical and commercial foundation with Google Cloud. In 2019, its international data center began supporting Google Cloud’s Partner Interconnect, enabling enterprise customers to reach Google Cloud edge points via the Chunghwa Telecom Global EXchange Center with bandwidth options ranging from 50 Mbps up to 10 Gbps. By mid-2021, Chunghwa Telecom advanced to Google Cloud Premier Partner status, formalizing a reseller and solutions role that bundled consulting, Interconnect, migrations, and managed services. The company positioned these offerings alongside its ICT capabilities in big data, IoT, and AI to support enterprise digital transformation, AI/ML workloads, and hybrid cloud architectures.
