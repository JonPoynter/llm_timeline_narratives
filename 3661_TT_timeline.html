Regulatory shock and realignment

2021-04-14 → 2024-12-31
Alchip’s AI-centric ASIC business weathered a major regulatory disruption in 2021 when a key China customer, Phytium, was added to the US entity list, forcing an immediate halt to shipments and creating significant near-term revenue risk. This episode catalyzed a strategic refocus toward North American hyperscalers and advanced-node programs. By the end of 2024, the company reported record financial performance concentrated in leading-edge nodes and North American demand, underscoring a successful pivot toward AI/HPC customers in the US cloud ecosystem.
