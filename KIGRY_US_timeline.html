Cloud AI foundations with Google Cloud (Dematic)

2022-06-06 → 2023-10-02
KION Group, through its Dematic subsidiary, established a foundational partnership with Google Cloud to accelerate cloud, AI, and ML-enabled modernization of warehouse and supply chain software. The initial 2022 announcement framed the collaboration as a strategic move to migrate Dematic offerings to the cloud and co-develop AI/ML capabilities. In 2023, Dematic operationalized elements of this strategy: a VividCloud case study documented Dematic’s migration toward a cloud-based microservices architecture on Google Cloud Platform (GCP), and Dematic launched its Sprocket Enterprise Asset Management software on Google Cloud Marketplace, creating a new distribution and deployment channel.
