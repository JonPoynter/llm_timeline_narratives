Foundation and benchmarking build-out

2018-03-06 → 2024-12-31
S&P Global established its modern AI foundation with the acquisition of Kensho in 2018, bringing core AI/ML, NLP, and data engineering capabilities in-house. This deal set the stage for internal productivity gains and external product innovation across divisions. In 2024, SPGI began productizing AI for customers: launching a generative AI-enabled search on S&P Global Marketplace and introducing S&P AI Benchmarks by Kensho to evaluate LLMs on finance tasks. Kensho also released an LLM-ready API (open beta) to make SPGI’s structured data accessible for generative AI, designed to interoperate with leading models (e.g., GPT, Gemini, Claude). A public leaderboard showcased multi-model performance, underscoring SPGI’s role as a neutral evaluator rather than a single-provider bet.
