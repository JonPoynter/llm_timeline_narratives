Foundation of Titan Intelligence and early Azure/OpenAI build-out

2022-04-22 → 2024-10-01
ServiceTitan launched Titan Intelligence (TI) in April 2022 as a suite of AI capabilities embedded across its platform, introducing features such as Smart Dispatch (Beta), Price Insights, and Marketing Pro Ads (Beta). This established an internal AI product foundation focused on trade-specific workflows rather than generic tooling. By mid-2024, ServiceTitan publicly detailed a generative AI application, Second Chance Leads, built on Microsoft Azure AI using Azure OpenAI Service and Azure AI prompt flow, indicating the use of third‑party LLMs (OpenAI via Azure) for production features. In October 2024 at Pantheon, the company unveiled an AI assistant spanning its suite alongside Sales Pro and call center applications, all under the Titan Intelligence brand and with partner Siro powering some capabilities—signaling continued expansion of AI features across customer-facing workflows.
