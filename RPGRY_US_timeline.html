Early GenAI experiments and internal enablement

REA Group began hands-on experimentation with OpenAI’s API in mid‑2023, applying generative AI to consumer UI elements on realestate.com.au to test engagement lifts. The team rapidly iterated within production constraints, gating volumes to respect OpenAI API rate limits while validating the fidelity of generated content. Through 2024 the company also showcased internal AI literacy and tooling via creative initiatives, including an AI‑curated art installation that explicitly used Adobe Firefly and ChatGPT Vision. These efforts signaled cultural adoption and experimentation across customer‑facing and internal brand experiences ahead of broader platform integrations.
