Foundational Chat AI and early automotive integrations

2023-03-07 → 2023-12-31
In early 2023 SoundHound introduced its generative AI orchestration architecture, unveiling CAILAN and CAINET to arbitrate between internal models and external LLMs such as OpenAI’s ChatGPT. Within weeks, SoundHound launched the Chat AI platform and consumer apps, positioning its approach to combine real-time knowledge domains with third-party generative models to mitigate hallucinations. By April 2023, SoundHound extended Chat AI into automotive, announcing an in-vehicle assistant able to integrate with any third-party generative model. Through late 2023, Stellantis’ DS Automobiles highlighted SoundHound AI powered by ChatGPT API in Europe, marking one of the first public automotive brand references to this integration.
