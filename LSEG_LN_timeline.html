Foundation with Microsoft; early gen-AI pilots

2022-12-12 → 2024-12-01

Through 2024, LSEG moved from platform groundwork to external pilots and user-facing enhancements. In December 2024, Microsoft introduced the Financial Meeting Prep app in Teams, powered by LSEG Workspace data and generative AI, to automate meeting preparation and email drafting for financial professionals.
