Foundational Google Cloud telco-edge AI collaboration

2021-06-29 → 2023-09-20
Ericsson and Google Cloud established a foundational partnership to co-develop 5G and edge cloud solutions for CSPs and enterprises, explicitly linking 5G with AI/ML at the edge. Initial work included onboarding Ericsson 5G on Anthos and live edge pilots, framing AI as a core enabler for automated, cloud-native telecom workloads. By late 2023 the collaboration expanded to Cloud RAN on Google Distributed Cloud (GDC) with integrated automation and orchestration, and explicit leverage of Google Cloud AI/ML services like Vertex AI and BigQuery to operationalize massive RAN data sets. This period set the technical and commercial groundwork for AI-driven, cloud-delivered network functions that would be productized in subsequent years.
