Agent Pay launch and ecosystem expansion

2025-04-29 → 2025-09-30
In 2025, Mastercard formally launched Agent Pay and positioned itself at the center of agentic commerce. The firm aligned with Microsoft to integrate Azure OpenAI Service and Copilot Studio, and introduced developer tooling (Agent Toolkit, MCP server) to make Mastercard APIs agent-ready. By September, Mastercard highlighted collaborations with Stripe, Google and others to scale secure agentic transactions. Importantly, OpenAI launched Instant Checkout in ChatGPT using the Agentic Commerce Protocol, enabling Mastercard Agent Pay and Agentic Tokens to operate on OpenAI’s platform for the first time.
