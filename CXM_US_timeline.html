Azure OpenAI migration; multi-provider expansion and AI operating economics

2026-01-01 → 2026-06-30
In 2026, Sprinklr operationalized a migration of OpenAI usage to Azure OpenAI per the 26.1 release, and expanded multi-provider capabilities across Vertex and Amazon Bedrock. Documentation introduced a provider capability matrix and BYOK integrations for Anthropic and xAI.
