Foundational alliances and LLM partnerships

2023-07-27 → 2023-10-22
SK Telecom initiated its AI strategy by co-founding the Global Telco AI Alliance (GTAA) in July 2023, committing to co-develop a Telco AI Platform with other major operators. Shortly thereafter, SKT deepened its AI posture with a $100 million strategic investment in Anthropic, pairing capital with a commercial plan to co-develop a multilingual, telco‑specific LLM. Complementing these moves, SKT engaged OpenAI in community and developer outreach through a joint AI hackathon in Korea. The company then formalized a broader, multi‑LLM strategy to blend in‑house models with external partners (Anthropic, OpenAI, and others), and partnered with Deutsche Telekom to co‑develop a telco‑specific LLM collaborating with Anthropic (Claude 2) and Meta (Llama2), with an initial version planned for early 2024. Together these steps established SKT’s strategic options across industry alliances, model providers, and commercialization pathways for AI-enhanced telco operations and customer experiences.
