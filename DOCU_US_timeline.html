Foundational LLM integrations via Azure OpenAI; early ChatGPT pathway

2023-05-23 → 2025-10-30
Docusign’s AI journey surfaced publicly in 2023 through Microsoft’s Build keynote and an ensuing earnings call, where the company highlighted Agreement Summarization powered by Microsoft’s Azure OpenAI Service. These steps established baseline product capabilities for extracting key datapoints and summarizing contracts, while reinforcing a deepening relationship with Microsoft and OpenAI technology via Azure. By late 2025, Docusign announced that its Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) capabilities would be accessible directly in ChatGPT via the Model Context Protocol (MCP), signaling a strategic shift toward multi‑agent, natural‑language workflows executed in third‑party AI interfaces. This created a direct integration surface with OpenAI’s ecosystem, expanding distribution and potential usage while beginning to externalize IAM actions beyond Docusign’s native UI.
