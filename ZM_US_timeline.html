Foundational federated AI approach and early model partnerships

2023-03-27 → 2023-12-31
Zoom began formalizing its generative AI strategy in 2023 with the expansion of Zoom IQ and an explicit commitment to a "federated approach to AI" that can route tasks across Zoom's own models and leading third‑party LLMs. Early execution included naming OpenAI as a model provider and announcing a strategic partnership and investment in Anthropic to bring Claude into the Zoom platform, starting with Contact Center. By September 2023, Zoom rebranded and broadened its generative assistant as Zoom AI Companion, making it available at no additional cost to paid accounts. Public technical updates through late 2023 documented active use of OpenAI's GPT‑3.5/4 and Anthropic Claude 2, reinforcing the multi‑model strategy and laying groundwork for broader adoption.
