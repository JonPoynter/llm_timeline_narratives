2026 Q1 record orders; AI/HBM momentum and product shifts

2026-05-07 → 2026-06-27
SUSS MicroTec reported record Q1 2026 order intake of €149.3m (book-to-bill 1.73), driven by AI-related modules—especially bonding for HBM—and broader mainstream demand. Management expects Q2 orders to exceed the record Q1 and noted an emphasis shift toward wafer-to-wafer hybrid bonding. Operationally, SUSS is ramping capacity in Taiwan and Sternenfels, with mid-end cleaner orders already in hand for deliveries starting in 2026 and spanning into 2027, and the first volume MaskTrack Smart system on order. Risks include a large customer not yet reordering, input cost pressures, and competitive dynamics as front-end players converge on back-end processes.
