Foundation in mission-critical data centers

2014-04-18 → 2021-12-14
EMCOR’s historical capabilities in mission-critical data center construction were established well before the recent AI surge. Dynalectric, an EMCOR company, featured prominently in large-scale data center projects such as COPT DC-6 in Manassas, Virginia, underscoring hands-on expertise in high-security, high-power infrastructure. By 2021, industry reporting highlighted Dynalectric D.C.’s extensive portfolio—nearly 1,700 MW of data centers—serving hyperscale and enterprise customers like Amazon, Microsoft, Facebook, and Alphabet under strict non-disclosure norms. This backdrop set the stage for EMCOR’s exposure to AI-era demand as hyperscalers and cloud leaders accelerated buildouts.
