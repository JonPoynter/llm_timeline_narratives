Anthropic partnership, multi‑provider posture, and FY26 results

2026-03-27 → 2026-06-30
Xero and Anthropic entered a multi-year partnership to integrate Claude into Xero and expose Xero financial data and tools within Claude.ai. A live integration followed quickly in May 2026, allowing Xero subscribers to bring their data into Claude conversations and link actions back into Xero. Xero reiterated a multi‑provider approach, augmenting its existing OpenAI relationship. FY26 results highlighted scaled JAX usage: 40m+ bank transactions reconciled at ~97% accuracy, 500k users of GenAI-specific features, and ~115% growth in JAX messages per user. Management emphasized that FY27 guidance assumed minimal direct AI monetization near term, while leaning on AI-enabled productivity to manage headcount and introducing the XeroForce agent builder (alpha). Xero also formally listed Anthropic and OpenAI as conversational AI subprocessors and published data-protection terms for JAX and developer tooling for Claude/OpenAI integration.
