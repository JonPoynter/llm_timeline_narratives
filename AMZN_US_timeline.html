Foundation of Bedrock, Anthropic tie-up, and AWS silicon ramp

2023-01-26 → 2023-11-28
Amazon’s AI posture began consolidating around Amazon Bedrock and a strategic alignment with Anthropic, while AWS accelerated its custom silicon roadmap. In early 2023, Amazon signaled internal caution around third-party chatbots like ChatGPT to protect code and confidential data. By April–September 2023, AWS launched Bedrock (initially in limited preview and then generally available), positioned to aggregate multiple frontier models behind a managed API, and announced a strategic collaboration where Anthropic selected AWS as its primary cloud and committed to Trainium/Inferentia, alongside Amazon’s up-to-$4B minority investment. Through November 2023, AWS broadened Bedrock’s model lineup (e.g., Meta’s Llama 2) and unveiled next-gen chips (Graviton4, Trainium2) targeting price/performance and scale gains for training and inference. These moves established a multi-model, chip-enabled foundation for Amazon’s GenAI strategy.
