LoRa ecosystem foundation and cloud hooks

2016-09-29 → 2024-03-28
Semtech’s AI-adjacent positioning originated in its LoRa/IoT ecosystem leadership, participating in the LoRa Alliance and showcasing LoRaWAN solutions as early as 2016. The ecosystem subsequently attracted major cloud participation, with Google Cloud joining the LoRa Alliance in 2018 and featuring LoRa in its Next ’18 IoT sessions. Competitive tension from cellular IoT carriers was already visible in 2018, as industry coverage framed LoRa as contending with licensed-spectrum alternatives. In 2022, Semtech expanded cloud touchpoints by integrating LoRa Cloud geolocation services into AWS IoT Core Device Location across seven regions, creating a device-to-cloud pathway for low-power asset tracking. The 2024 10-K reiterates Semtech’s core markets across data centers and connectivity hardware, setting up the company’s later AI data center interconnect push.
