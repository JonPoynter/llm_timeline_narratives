Foundational platform integrations with Meta and Google

2022-11-02 → 2024-04-09
LiveRamp began deepening AI-adjacent integrations across major walled gardens, starting with Meta’s Conversions API support and an expanded partnership built on RampID and ATS. The Google relationship extended across PAIR, Ads/ADH, and cloud-embedded identity, positioning LiveRamp’s clean room and identity stack as connective tissue for marketers operating in a post-cookie world.
