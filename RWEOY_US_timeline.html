2025 expansion: more data center PPAs and AWS collaboration

2025-03-01 → 2025-12-31
RWE broadened its AI-adjacent demand base in 2025 by adding data center PPAs in the UK and extending its Meta portfolio in Texas, while also formalizing a strategic cloud and AI collaboration with AWS. In March, RWE secured a 10-year PPA to power Telehouse’s London Docklands data center campus and announced a new 200 MWac PPA with Meta for Waterloo Solar in Texas. The companies noted seven prior U.S. PPAs totaling 1.1 GW supporting Amazon’s operations, underscoring the scale of RWE’s hyperscaler relationships.

2024 hyperscaler PPAs kick-off

2024-05-01 → 2024-12-31
RWE began cementing AI-adjacent power demand exposure through major corporate PPAs with hyperscalers. In May 2024, the company signed two 15-year PPAs with Microsoft for a combined 446 MW of onshore wind in Texas, directly linking generation growth to cloud and AI infrastructure power needs. In August 2024, RWE added Meta as a long-term offtaker for 374 MW across two U.S. solar farms, expanding its pipeline of data-center-aligned contracts. These agreements positioned RWE as a key renewable supplier to technology companies scaling AI workloads, with multi-hundred-megawatt capacities and multi-year tenors that increase visibility into AI-driven power demand growth.
