Foundations: GreenLake for LLMs, Aleph Alpha tie-up, and space-based LLM demo

2023-06-20 → 2024-08-01
HPE entered the AI cloud services market by launching GreenLake for Large Language Models, positioning to let enterprises privately train, tune, and deploy LLMs on HPE supercomputing infrastructure with sustainability attributes and colocation delivery. The initial service highlighted a partnership with Aleph Alpha and early North American delivery via QScale facilities running on nearly 100% renewable energy. HPE deepened its linkage to Aleph Alpha later in 2023 via Hewlett Packard Pathfinder joining Aleph Alpha’s more than $500 million Series B round, underscoring a strategic focus on sovereign and trustworthy AI. In 2024, HPE’s Spaceborne Computer-2 aboard the International Space Station hosted a generative AI LLM experiment led by Booz Allen Hamilton, demonstrating edge AI potential in constrained environments and expanding HPE’s credibility in specialized AI deployments.
