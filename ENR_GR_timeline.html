Early hyperscaler renewable power ties

2018-05-25 → 2019-11-07
Before the current AI-driven spike in data center demand, Siemens-affiliated energy businesses supplied renewable generation and grid works tied to hyperscaler data centers in Europe. In 2018, Siemens Gamesa turbines were selected to power Facebook's Nordic data centers via a 294 MW PPA. In 2019, Siemens Gamesa and Siemens Gas and Power participated in the 254 MW Stavro wind farm in Sweden, with power sold through corporate offtakes including Google, and grid connection delivered by Siemens Gas and Power. These legacy projects demonstrate early exposure to hyperscaler infrastructure powering large-scale compute, laying groundwork for the firm's later focus on AI data center power needs.
