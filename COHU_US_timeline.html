2026-02-17 → 2026-06-27
Cohu entered 2026 with rising AI/HPC momentum across handlers, inspection, and software analytics. The company announced multi‑unit Eclipse orders tied to next‑generation AI datacenter processors and $30 million in follow‑on Eclipse orders, including adoption of PAICe Prescriptive analytics subscriptions. AI software foundation and analytics expansion

2023-11-16 → 2024-12-31
Cohu initiated its modern AI software push by introducing DI-Core AI Inspection in late 2023 to improve visual inspection accuracy at production speeds using deep-learning techniques. The launch targeted a recurring software model, highlighting an opportunity for approximately $5 million in annual subscription revenue and reported initial orders from two customers. In December 2024, Cohu signed a definitive agreement to acquire Tignis to enhance its AI/ML-based process control and analytics. The deal was aimed at expanding DI-Core with PAICe Monitor and PAICe Maker capabilities, addressing an estimated $2.6 billion semiconductor process control market. Financial terms were not disclosed and were stated as not material to Cohu’s financials.
