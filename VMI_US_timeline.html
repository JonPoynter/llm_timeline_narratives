Foundational AI partnership and acquisition (Valley + Prospera)

2019-02-20 → 2021-07-21
Valmont, through its Valley Irrigation business, initiated its AI journey via an exclusive global partnership with Prospera Technologies to integrate machine vision and AI with center pivot irrigation systems. The partners launched anomaly detection capabilities and quickly scaled coverage beyond initial targets, indicating strong early customer traction. By 2021, Valmont elected to internalize these AI capabilities by acquiring Prospera for approximately $300 million, transitioning its AI expertise from partnership to wholly owned subsidiary. The acquisition aligned with a strategy to embed real-time crop analysis and anomaly detection into Valley’s connected irrigation footprint and accelerate technology-enabled growth.
