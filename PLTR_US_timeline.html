AIP launch and early activation

2023-04-07 → 2023-12-31
Palantir publicly introduced its Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP) as a fourth platform alongside Gotham, Foundry, and Apollo, committing to an initial release to strategic partners by late May 2023. This marked the company's formal entry into enabling large language model (LLM) use within governed, operational workflows across commercial and government sectors. By June 2023, Palantir began enabling AIP features inside Foundry enrollments, including AIP Assist, an LLM-powered support tool trained on Palantir documentation. This established a pattern of integrating third-party LLMs under Palantir's security model while making AI capabilities accessible inside existing customer environments.
