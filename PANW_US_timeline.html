2026-04-22 → 2026-06-11
In spring 2026, PANW advanced deep integrations with frontier AI providers. With Google Cloud it launched Prisma AIRS Model Security as a native agent in the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform and joined the Agent‑as‑a‑Service marketplace. PANW also appeared as a launch partner in Anthropic’s expanded Project Glasswing.
