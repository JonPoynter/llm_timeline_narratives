On-device, Android partnership, watermarking, and 26Q1 agentic pivot

2026-02-12 → 2026-06-16
In early 2026 Kakao deepened platformization: partnering with Google to optimize on-device AI services (beginning with Kanana in KakaoTalk) and explore Android XR AI glasses; extending PlayMCP by linking with OpenClaw; and adopting Google DeepMind’s SynthID watermarking for responsible AI in Kanana’s image/video models. These moves reinforced both consumer integration and governance controls. The 26Q1 filing detailed an accelerated pivot to an agentic AI platform on KakaoTalk with early engagement metrics, internal/external Kanana integrations, and timelines for agentic commerce. In June, Kakao added a ChatGPT Chatbot for real-time answers directly inside KakaoTalk chat rooms, further mainstreaming AI within core messaging UX.

Consumer ChatGPT integration and developer tooling

2025-10-28 → 2025-11-24
Kakao rolled out ChatGPT for Kakao in October 2025, enabling direct ChatGPT use within KakaoTalk for its 50 million users and introducing the Kakao Tools agent to connect to services like KakaoMap, KakaoTalk Gift, and Melon. Toolbox allowed users to manage MCP tools and connect them into external AI services such as ChatGPT and Claude via single Kakao account authentication—advancing a multi-model, interoperable tooling strategy.
