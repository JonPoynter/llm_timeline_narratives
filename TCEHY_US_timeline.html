Regulatory headwinds on foreign LLMs in WeChat

2022-12-28 → 2023-02-22
China’s regulatory stance on foreign chatbots directly affected Tencent’s WeChat platform. In late 2022, ChatGPT-related mini-programs and access points were removed from WeChat, signaling enforcement actions on content deemed outside the platform’s permitted scope. By February 2023, reports indicated regulators told large platforms, including Tencent, not to offer ChatGPT services directly or via third parties and to restrict access. This set boundary conditions for Tencent’s AI roadmap inside WeChat/Weixin, pushing the company toward domestic or self-developed models.
