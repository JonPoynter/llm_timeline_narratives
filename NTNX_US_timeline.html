2026-02-25 → 2026-06-30

Enterprise AI platform expansion and open‑model tooling

2024-11-12 → 2025-05-09
Nutanix extended its Enterprise AI (NAI) platform beyond on‑prem and edge to run in public clouds, aiming for a consistent hybrid multicloud operating model for accelerated AI workloads. Technical publications detailed how to deploy new "reasoning"-oriented models efficiently on Nutanix, including DeepSeek R1‑derived Llama variants, and highlighted OpenAI API compatibility in NAI to simplify developer integration. Nutanix also published guidance on enterprise data privacy risks of public LLM services, underscoring the rationale for controlled, customer‑owned AI infrastructure.

Foundational Enterprise AI offerings and early traction

2023-08-15 → 2024-05-21
Nutanix introduced its first packaged generative AI offering, GPT-in-a-Box, positioning the Nutanix Cloud Platform as an on‑prem and edge foundation for fine‑tuning and running LLMs while keeping customer data under control. The launch emphasized curating open-source models and MLOps frameworks, and specifically cited support for Meta’s Llama family alongside other open models. Through late 2023, management disclosed the first GPT-in-a-Box customer win at a U.S. federal agency, noting the product was still early and not yet a material revenue driver. In May 2024 at .NEXT, Nutanix expanded functionality via integrations with NVIDIA NIM and Hugging Face and announced an AI Partner Program, further building an ecosystem to speed time-to-value for on‑prem generative AI use cases.
