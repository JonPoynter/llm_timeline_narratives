Box AI launch and multi‑cloud model integrations

2023-05-02 → 2024-10-24

By October 2024, Box deepened its multi‑model stance with an AWS partnership to access Amazon Bedrock models, starting with Anthropic’s Claude and Amazon Titan, and introduced a connector for Amazon Q.

Pre‑GenAI groundwork and skills framework

2017-10-11 → 2023-04-30
Box laid early foundations for AI in content management with the introduction of Box Skills in 2017, a framework to apply third‑party machine learning to Box‑stored content. Demonstrations at BoxWorks 2017 showed integrations with major AI ecosystems including IBM Watson, Microsoft Azure Cognitive Services, and Google Cloud. This period established Box’s long‑running pattern of partnering with multiple AI providers rather than building proprietary foundation models, foreshadowing the later multi‑model strategy that underpins Box AI.
