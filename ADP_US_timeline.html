Foundations, early gen-AI rollout, and Salesforce integration

2018-06-25 → 2024-06-10
ADP’s enterprise AI journey traces public roots to at least 2018, when the company highlighted machine learning and AI efforts around its data assets and ecosystem partners at an internal Datathon. This period established ADP’s data culture and partner collaborations as the groundwork for later generative AI initiatives. By late 2023 and early 2024, ADP began explicitly productizing gen‑AI in client‑facing and service workflows. The December 2023 expansion with Salesforce brought generative AI into ADP’s service operations to accelerate knowledge retrieval for support teams. In early 2024, ADP rolled out ADP Assist in early release, describing a hybrid approach that uses external and proprietary LLMs deployed in ADP’s private instances across AWS and/or Microsoft Azure — with models not trained on ADP data. In June 2024, ADP reiterated that ADP Assist had launched earlier in the year as a cross‑platform gen‑AI solution.
