By December 2024, PTC announced a three-way collaboration with Microsoft and Volkswagen Group to co-develop a generative AI copilot for Codebeamer, embedding Microsoft Azure AI capabilities. These moves positioned PTC’s CAD/PLM/ALM stack to incorporate generative AI assistants powered by major cloud platforms and to seed customer co-innovation programs.
