Foundational Android Automotive and early AI partnerships

2018-05-29 → 2022-02-10
Aptiv established itself as a key integrator for Google's Android Automotive OS, beginning with co-development efforts alongside Volvo and Google to bring next‑generation infotainment and connectivity into production vehicles. These integrations emphasized built‑in access to Google's apps and services, voice assistance via Google Assistant, and over‑the‑air update capabilities, culminating in early production launches on Polestar 2 and Volvo XC40 and recognition via a CLEPA Innovation Award. In parallel, Aptiv deepened its AI capabilities through partnerships and device leadership roles, including a minority investment in Affectiva to enhance in‑cabin human perception AI and serving as a lead device partner with Google for Android Automotive — providing reference hardware platforms and integration expertise. By 2022, Aptiv was positioning Android Automotive as a platform for commercial and fleet applications, highlighting its role in enabling a more engaging and efficient driver experience.
