Conversational search with Gemini; ChatGPT app; FY25 results posture and AI operating model

2026-02-12 → 2026-05-08
Rightmove launched a beta of AI-powered conversational property search on its site in February 2026, built with Google’s Gemini models in close collaboration with Google Cloud. Two weeks later, the firm submitted an experimental app to launch within ChatGPT to test discovery behaviors in conversational environments. By March 2026, Rightmove became the first UK property portal to have a live app in ChatGPT. The FY25 results in late February laid out the broader AI posture: 31 live AI initiatives, a vendor-agnostic (currently Gemini) architecture with the ability to swap models, explicit LLM optionality and data custody for the ChatGPT app, and early engagement signals (circa 3x higher lead propensity) albeit with caution on causality. Management noted rapid token cost deflation (80–90% annually) and framed compute as a cost-to-deliver opportunity, but guided to lower margins in 2026 due to step-up AI investment. They also downplayed immediate competitive risk from ChatGPT traffic for home search, while emphasizing controls to block or allow LLM access.
