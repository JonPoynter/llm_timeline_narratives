US advanced packaging anchors and Arizona ecosystem formation

2023-11-30 → 2024-11-14
From late 2023 through 2024, Amkor established foundational U.S. advanced packaging commitments led by Apple’s anchor-customer role for a new Arizona facility and a collaboration framework with TSMC to localize leading-edge packaging. These steps positioned Amkor as a key back-end partner adjacent to front-end fabs in Phoenix, matching U.S. onshoring initiatives and customers’ AI compute roadmaps. Partnership breadth expanded to cover heterogeneous integration for AI accelerators and photonic computing: Lightmatter selected Amkor to co-develop what it called the world’s largest 3D photonics package. Taken together, these moves increased Amkor’s strategic relevance in the AI supply chain without tying the company to any single LLM provider.
