Financing closure, Beacon Point commercialization, and Q1’26 expansion of contracted AI capacity

2026-04-29 → 2026-05-26
Ahead of its Q1 2026 release, Hut 8 closed $3.25 billion in investment-grade, fully amortizing notes to finance River Bend, reinforcing the Google-backed, Fluidstack-operated 245 MW lease and providing capital for construction and substations. On May 6, 2026, Hut 8 commercialized the first phase of its 1 GW Beacon Point campus in Texas via a 15-year, 352 MW lease with a high-investment-grade tenant valued at $9.8 billion, bringing the contracted AI lease base across Beacon Point and River Bend to ~$16.8 billion and 597 MW IT. Management detailed a power-first execution model, densification with NVIDIA as a design partner (lifting Beacon Point’s base-term value by ~$3.6 billion), ROFO and exclusivity rights for the tenant, and multiple project milestones (Entergy switchyard timing, Beacon Point delivery phasing, River Bend hall timelines). Q1 highlights also flagged risks around build/interconnect timing, counterparty concentration, and regulatory/power market exposures in Texas and Louisiana. Subsequently, Third Point disclosed a new equity position in Hut 8 aligned with its AI data center transition thesis.

GPU-as-a-Service launch and Anthropic/Fluidstack partnership formation

2024-09-26 → 2025-12-17
Hut 8 initiated its AI transition with the launch of a GPU-as-a-service vertical in September 2024, deploying a 1,000 NVIDIA H100 cluster at a Chicago facility under its Highrise AI subsidiary. This marked the first revenue from Hut 8's AI compute offerings and established operational credibility with HPE Cray supercomputing gear. By December 2025, Hut 8 formalized a major AI infrastructure partnership with Anthropic and Fluidstack, outlining a path from an initial 245 MW to potentially 2,295 MW of AI data center capacity. Concurrently, the company executed a definitive 15-year, $7.0 billion River Bend lease with Fluidstack, backed by a Google financial backstop, creating a foundational, investment-grade contracted revenue pillar for Hut 8's AI data center strategy.
