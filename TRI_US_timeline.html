TR’s 26Q1 disclosures emphasized internal LLM progress and cost leverage alongside active multi-model optionality. Foundations: Casetext GPT-4 lineage and first TR GenAI integrations

2023-03-14 → 2023-11-15
Thomson Reuters’ AI journey for legal workflows traces to Casetext’s March 2023 launch of CoCounsel powered by OpenAI’s GPT-4. Thomson Reuters moved quickly to secure these capabilities by signing a definitive agreement in June 2023 to acquire Casetext for $650 million in cash, closing in August 2023. This brought CoCounsel and its GPT-4 lineage fully in-house. By November 2023, Thomson Reuters launched generative AI-powered solutions, including AI-Assisted Research on Westlaw Precision and a unified platform strategy designed to scale across multiple LLM providers, while integrating CoCounsel across its legal portfolio. These steps established early product integration and distribution foundations for GenAI within the company’s core franchises.
