In 2023, Unity formally moved into AI-enablement for creators, launching both an AI marketplace in the Unity Asset Store and debuting Unity Sentis and Unity Muse to accelerate content creation and runtime inference in RT3D projects. These steps marked Unity's initial platformization of AI for developers and studios.
