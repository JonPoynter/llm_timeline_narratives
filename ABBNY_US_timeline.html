AI-driven data center and grid momentum; 26Q1 posture

2026-04-22 → 2026-04-22
ABB’s 26Q1 update emphasized strong AI-adjacent demand from data centers and grid investments. Management cited double-digit order growth across business areas, highlighted ABB’s position as the first and only provider of a medium-voltage UPS, and noted robust order quality with down payments. They indicated most recent data center orders would deliver over 12–24 months. Financially, ABB reported a group backlog of $27.5bn (+27% y/y) with Electrification backlog at $11.5bn (+40%). The company plans higher CapEx primarily in Electrification and maintains approximately $18B of M&A firepower, including adjacencies in data centers and grid automation. Management saw no signs of panic buying but flagged that concentration in data centers and grid could impact order conversion if momentum slows.
