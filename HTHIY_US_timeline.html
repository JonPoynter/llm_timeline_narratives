Foundations: Generative AI centers and first hyperscaler alliances

2023-05-15 → 2024-06-03
Hitachi began formalizing its generative AI strategy in mid-2023 by launching a Generative AI Center and deploying internal tools built on Azure OpenAI Service, in collaboration with Microsoft Japan. Through subsidiaries, Hitachi also introduced Azure OpenAI-enabled services to support low-code application development, signaling an early commercialization path. Concurrently, Hitachi Energy and Google Cloud initiated a strategic agreement that explicitly referenced generative AI. In 2024, Hitachi announced a Generative AI Common Platform spanning Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service, GitHub Copilot, and open-source LLMs, and separately unveiled a multi‑year strategic alliance with Google Cloud centered on Gemini/Vertex AI. The phase culminated with a three‑year, projected multi‑billion‑dollar Microsoft partnership to embed Azure Open AI Service and Copilots across Lumada and the workforce.
