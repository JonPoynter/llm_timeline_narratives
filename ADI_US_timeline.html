AI infrastructure ramp accelerates; hyperscaler-facing power and optical scale

2026-01-01 → 2026-06-27
ADI’s 2025 Annual Report and 1H26 earnings emphasized rapidly growing AI-driven data center demand for advanced power delivery and high-speed connectivity. Q1 and Q2 highlighted record data center orders, data center exceeding 75% of Communications with >90% y/y growth, and continued balance between power and optical. Management also cautioned that hyperscaler capex visibility remains limited and episodic. Strategically, ADI moved to acquire Empower Semiconductor for $1.5B to expand high-density power delivery (IVR and silicon capacitors) for AI compute, with modest revenue impact expected in 2H26 and more significant contribution in 2027. The company joined NVIDIA’s MGX ecosystem and reinforced 800 VDC rack-level architecture support as AI factories trend toward megawatt-class racks. Capacity plans reference support for up to $20B of revenue toward its 2030 vision.
