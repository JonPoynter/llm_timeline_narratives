FY26 scaling with multi-provider programs; bookings and M&A mixed signals

2026-02-26 → 2026-06-27
In 1H FY26, Accenture expanded its multi-model posture and productized security operations. It announced a multi-year collaboration with Mistral AI; launched Cyber.AI, powered by Anthropic’s Claude, reporting significant internal efficiency gains; and deepened its Google Cloud alliance via the Gemini Enterprise Acceleration Program with early access to Google DeepMind’s frontier models. Accenture Federal Services partnered with OpenAI to speed secure AI adoption across US federal agencies, enabling broad, governed model access for practitioners. Q3 FY26 reported $19.3B bookings with a 1.0 book-to-bill, but down ~2% y/y and sequentially below Q2. Management highlighted ecosystem partner revenue now exceeding 60% of total and AI/data bookings with emerging partners on track to more than double year over year. Accenture noted it is on track to more than double bookings from emerging AI/data partners including Anthropic, Gemini, OpenAI, Mistral AI, Nvidia, Palantir, Databricks, and Snowflake, and joined OpenAI’s Daybreak Cyber Partner Program.
