Hyperscale entry and JV formation momentum

2025-01-16 → 2025-07-15
PPL’s Kentucky utility (LG&E/KU) announced its first hyperscale data center electric customer with a 400 MW campus in Louisville, including staged availability starting in 2026 and access to 335 MW with expansion to 402 MW. This marked the company’s initial, concrete hyperscale load in its portfolio. Concurrently, regional ecosystem developments accelerated. AWS and Talen finalized a 17‑year, $18B PPA to supply up to 1,920 MW to AWS data centers in Pennsylvania, with PPL Electric delivering power over the grid. PPL and Blackstone then formed a JV (July 15, 2025) to build, own, and operate new gas-fired combined-cycle plants under long-term ESAs to serve Pennsylvania data centers, alongside Blackstone’s broader plan to invest over $25B in Pennsylvania’s digital and energy infrastructure.
